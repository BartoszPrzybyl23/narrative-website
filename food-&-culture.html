Food & Culture

Savour Croatia's diverse culinary heritage

Exploring Croatian Flavors

These dishes showcase Croatia's diverse culinary heritage, influenced by both inland and coastal traditions, and represent a fusion of Croatian flavours.

Croatian dishes

Peka

A traditional Croatian technique that involves slowly cooking vegetables and meat—typically lamb, veal, or octopus—under a bell-shaped dome over hot coals. Tender, succulent meat and flavorful veggies are produced by combining spices, herbs, and a peka (bell-shaped lid) with the cooking method.

Did you know?

Did you know that Croatia is home to the world's smallest town with a Michelin-starred restaurant? In the picturesque town of Hum, known as the smallest town in the world, you can find the restaurant "Humska Konoba," serving delightful traditional Croatian cuisine amidst the charming setting of this tiny historic town.


Cevapi

These are little sausages with minced meat that are grilled and seasoned with paprika, garlic, and other spices. The meat can be a combination of beef, lamb, or pork. They are usually served with chopped onions, a side of ajvar (a red pepper-based condiment), and flatbread (like somun or lepinja).

Octopus Salad (Salata od Hobota)

An appetiser that is light and refreshing, made from cooked octopus that is typically marinated with potatoes, onions, garlic, vinegar, and olive oil. This dish is a coastal favourite in Croatia, with a delicious hint of sea flavour.

Pasticada

A beef dish cooked slowly, flavoured with cloves and other spices, marinated in wine vinegar and garlic, and stewed with prunes and prošek (sweet dessert wine). It's cooked until soft and served with mashed potatoes, gnocchi, or homemade pasta all of which have a savoury, rich flavour.

Fritule

Light, fluffy fritters or doughnuts, especially well-liked during Croatian holidays such as Christmas. Dough is combined with eggs, sugar, zest from citrus fruits, and occasionally brandy or raisins. These tiny candies are coated in powdered sugar and deep-fried, providing a delightful treat.
